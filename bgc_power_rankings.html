Heavy Favorite Tournament Pick
The Uncs are the clear #1 team in the field and it isn't particularly close. Grifdy's roster construction was the best in the draft by a wide margin — a former professional player (Lava) at the FDPS slot, a GM-caliber captain on tank, a Masters-level second DPS in Divina, and a high-Diamond support duo in HomieBaba and jisf. The $103,000 surplus between what was paid and true value speaks to the efficiency. The one question mark is whether Lava's transition from tank to DPS role fully materializes under match pressure — but elite mechanics travel, and at Champion 4 he's operating at a tier above anyone else in this tournament.

Power Score74
True Value$227k
74 Power
Contender Finals Threat
The Grinders are the only team in the field that can genuinely challenge the Uncs on any given map. RET at Champion 4 is the best support in the tournament by rank, and the toeinfection-RET support duo at 4000 + Champion 4 SR is a matchup nightmare for every roster in this field. Aussie's high Masters tank provides strong shot-calling, and the post-draft trade acquiring NotGunner adds flex depth that the original roster lacked. The gap to #1 is real — Grifdy's DPS line outclasses them — but the Grinders' support advantage is potent enough to steal a series.

Power Score65
True Value$186k
65 Power
Contender Semi Threat
The Forscythies are the most balanced team in the field — lexx's GM Lucio anchors one of the better support profiles in the tournament, starryflower provides a reliable high-Diamond tank, and the GoldenGear/NinerActual DPS pairing is Diamond-to-low-Masters caliber. The issue is the Forscythies have no transcendent talent to carry a map when it goes sideways. They beat the teams below them cleanly and push the teams above them competitively — a profile that almost always results in a strong group stage and a semifinal exit.

Sleeper Mafia-Dep.
Pratty's Pals are the most boom-or-bust team in the field. Mafia's GM Pharah/Tracer/Echo profile is a genuine x-factor — a player who can outright win maps singlehandedly against Diamond-tier rosters. If Crazerio performs at his corrected Masters level and Mafia is locked in, this team can knock anyone out on a good day. The risk is that the support depth behind Th0rnt0ns is thin, and if Mafia has an off game there isn't a secondary carry to pick up the slack. Ranked 4th but with the widest range of outcomes in the field.
5
Terabyte Titans
Captain: Tera  ·  Draft Grade D+
Power Score38
True Value$126k
38 Power
Underdog Aurum-Dep.
The Titans enter the tournament with the lowest power score in the field by a significant margin — a direct result of the most inefficient draft in the tournament. Aurum is a legitimate GM-level carry (Kiriko 4214, Emre 4034) and the post-draft trades landing jj183 (DPS depth) and BleedingLove (a fourth support body, acquired for caesarnox) show roster awareness. But no carry in a five-player game can compensate for a Bronze-tier and an unranked support soaking up $73,000 in combined salary. BleedingLove's console Lifeweaver onetrick profile helps the headcount but not the tier gap. The Titans will be competitive in maps where Aurum takes over completely; they will struggle badly in any map that requires coordinated support play. Projected last in the group stage.
